Crispy Chicken Popcorn Recipe
------
Chicken popcorn is a popular snack made of bite-sized pieces of chicken that are marinated, coated, and deep-fried until golden and crispy. To make it, take boneless chicken breast or thigh and cut it into small, bite-sized cubes about one inch in size. In a bowl, marinate the chicken pieces with salt, black pepper, red chili powder, garlic paste, a little soy sauce, and one beaten egg. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes so the flavors absorb into the chicken. In a separate plate, mix together all-purpose flour, cornflour, a pinch of baking soda, oregano, and more chili powder for extra heat. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying. Take each marinated chicken piece and roll it in the dry flour mixture until fully coated. For an extra crispy layer, dip it back into the egg mixture and then again into the flour. Carefully drop the coated chicken pieces into the hot oil and fry on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy, which takes about 3 to 4 minutes. Remove them with a slotted spoon and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil. Serve hot with ketchup, mayonnaise, or a spicy dip. Chicken popcorn is perfect as a party snack or a quick appetizer.
