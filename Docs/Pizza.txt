Simple Homemade Pizza Recipe
------
To make a simple homemade pizza, start with pizza dough, which you can buy ready-made or make from scratch using flour, yeast, warm water, salt, and a little olive oil. Roll the dough into a round circle about half an inch thick. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce or tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add your favorite toppings such as pepperoni, sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, or olives. For a basic margherita pizza, just use fresh basil leaves and slices of tomato. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible, usually around 250°C or 480°F. If you have a pizza stone, use it; otherwise, bake the pizza on a baking sheet for about 10 to 15 minutes until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned in spots. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a minute, then slice and serve hot. The result is a crispy, cheesy, and satisfying meal that beats any delivery pizza.
