Butter Cheese Sandwich Recipe
-----
To make a delicious butter cheese sandwich, start by taking two slices of bread. In a small bowl, mix softened butter with a pinch of salt (as needed), a sprinkle of oregano, a dash of chili flakes, and optionally some garlic powder for extra flavor. Stir everything well until the spices are evenly blended into the butter. Take one slice of bread and spread the prepared spiced butter generously on one side. Place this slice in a frying pan with the buttered side facing down, and turn the flame to low. Immediately add a generous layer of pizza cheese on top of the slice that is now facing up. Cover the cheese with the second slice of bread. Then, spread the remaining spiced butter on the top side of this second slice. Let the sandwich cook on low flame for about four minutes. After this time, carefully flip the sandwich using a spatula. The cooked side should ideally be golden brown – if it is, that side is done. If it appears only golden yellow, let it toast for another minute or so until it reaches a deeper brown color. Repeat the same process for the other side. Once both sides are perfectly golden brown and the cheese has melted, remove the sandwich from the pan. Serve hot.
